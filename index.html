Questions

What patients ask us

Do you treat injuries from a car accident?+
Yes. We evaluate and treat accident-related injuries such as whiplash, back and neck pain, and soft-tissue strain with chiropractic care, physical therapy, and rehabilitation, and we keep clear records of your care. Call (513) 435-0805 to be seen.
What is functional medicine and how is it different?+
Functional medicine looks for the root cause of a problem rather than only treating symptoms. We combine it with chiropractic, physical therapy, and sports medicine so your care addresses why you hurt, not just where.
